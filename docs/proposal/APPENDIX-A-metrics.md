# Technical appendix A: spine evaluation tables

*AI4I Challenge 2026, Track 3 Development. This appendix is not counted toward the 10 page limit (terms of reference, section 6). The tables are read from the committed metrics files, not retyped by hand. Referenced from Section 2.*

## Spine 1: arrival prediction

Method: leave one journey out. Every journey is held out in turn, both scorers are fit on the remaining journeys, and mean absolute error is measured on the held out segment durations. Route HEIGHTS-REZENDE, data source real field ride.

| Measure | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Recorded journeys | 2 |
| Segment observations | 20 |
| Baseline, per segment average (the named naive average) | 96.4 s over 20 predictions |
| Model, per segment per hour average | 96.4 s over 20 predictions |
| Verdict | insufficient_data |
| Served engine | baseline:v1 |

*Source: `services/spine/metrics/METRICS.md`, generated by `pnpm spine:train`. The model serves only when there are at least 10 recorded journeys and it beats the baseline on held out data; the rule lives in `src/eta/train.ts` and serving code reads only the verdict in `metrics.json`. The promotion path is proven on a synthetic rush hour fixture in `test/eta-train.test.ts`, where the model wins and is promoted.*

## Spine 3: revenue watchdog

Method: 10 independent synthetic replicates, each 84 training days and 36 held out days. Both detectors are fit on the training window only and scored on the held out tail against the generator's own ground truth labels. Data source synthetic, with the generator and its config committed at `src/watchdog/simulate.ts` and `src/watchdog/config.ts`. Held out days: 360, of which 22 carry injected leakage.

| Detector | Precision | Recall | F1 | TP | FP | FN |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fixed threshold (the named baseline, 60% drop rule) | never fired | 0.0% | 0 | 0 | 0 | 22 |
| Isolation forest over per route per day features | 73.9% | 77.3% | 0.756 | 17 | 6 | 5 |

*Source: `services/spine/metrics/WATCHDOG-METRICS.md`, generated by `pnpm watchdog:eval`. Verdict: promoted, served engine forest:v1. The threshold never fires because injected leakage hides inside one kombi of four, moving the route total by well under the 40 percent drop the threshold needs. Flags always describe a pattern, a day, a route, an unnamed vehicle, never a person, enforced by unit test on the explanation templates.*
